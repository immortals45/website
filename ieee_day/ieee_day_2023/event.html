IEEE Day, an annual celebration of technology and community engagement, has been a tradition since 1884. As a global community, IEEE transcends borders and federations, uniting all in a shared commitment to excellence.

In 2023, we proudly celebrate the 12th annual IEEE Day with the theme "EVOLVE: Elevate to Excellence" on October 5th. While IEEE Day is officially recognized on the first Tuesday in October, our festivities extend from September 25th to October 7th, ensuring ample opportunities for participation.

As IEEE representatives, we take pride in emphasizing the significance of IEEE and its mission. We continually expand our reach, fostering collaboration among IEEE members and colleagues worldwide. Join us in harnessing technology for a better tomorrow during this year's IEEE Day celebrations!
