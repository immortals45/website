About Us

IEEE CS

We as members of Computer Society empower people in technical advancement by delivering tools for individuals at all stages of their careers.As a face of IEEE Computer Society professional chapters ,we aid technology professionals stay active, involved, and engaged in community by providing a medium for networking and collaboration.

Mission

We as a part of professional society IEEE contribute to all technical and non-technical activities. Also sponser workshops, competitions, talks and publications. We strive to "Design and Shape the Technology of tomorrow."

What We Do?

IEEE Computer society sponsors workshops and conference

Publishes a variety of peer-reviewed literature.

Participates in educational activities at all level of professions.

Conducting various events for enhancing knowledge.
